Yummy MeatPies!

A yummy Meatpie best downed with chilled Fanta Orange Pop soda

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced beef
  • 1000g of Flour
  • 500g Margarine
  • 100g diced Potatoes
  • 100g diced carrots
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt to taste

Recipes Instructions

  1. Mix all dry Ingredients
  2. Add all wet ingredients to its dry components
  3. Mix Dough untill flakky
  4. Add the Margarine and mix dough thoroughly
  5. Roll out dough and cut into desired shapes
  6. Lay baking tray with foil paper and preheat oven at 400F
  7. Bake pies for 1hr or until brown

    8. Allow to cool and serve with chilled pop soda

    Bon Apetite!!!
