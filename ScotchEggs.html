Crunchy Scotch Eggs

A Crunchy scotch egg is best paired with Chapman mocktail on a sunny day!

This is the most sought after snack in Nigeria.
What a delight to indulge in this masterpiece, work of art by Aunty Mina.

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Sausage
  • 1000g of Flour
  • 500g Margarine
  • 1 big bulb of Onion
  • All purpose seasoning to taste
  • 3 scotch bonnet peppers for flame
  • Salt to taste

Recipes Instructions

  1. Mix all dry Ingredients
  2. Add all wet ingredients to its dry components
  3. Mix Dough untill flakky
  4. Add the Margarine and mix dough thoroughly
  5. Roll dough around cooled hard boild eggs
  6. Lay each roll on baking tray and preheat oven at 400F
  7. Bake rolls for 30mins or until brown

    8. Allow to cool and serve with chilled Chapman
