Signature Chapman Cocktail Drink

A chilled bottle of Aunty Mina's signature Chapman cocktail drink is the best thing on a sunny day!

This is the most sought after cocktail in Nigeria.
What a delight to indulge in this masterpiece, work of art by Aunty Mina.

Ingredients

  • 330ml Fanta orange pop soda
  • 330m Sprite pop soda
  • 50ml Angustura Bitters
  • Slices of Lemon
  • Slices of Cucumber
  • Ice cubes to serve

Recipes Instructions

  1. Mix all the drinks in a jar
  2. pour into cocktail glass
  3. Add slics of lemon and cucumber to garnish
  4. Add Ice cubes and serve chilled

    5. Bon Apetite!!! Life is Good!
